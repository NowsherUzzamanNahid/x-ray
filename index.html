FAQ



Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use 1 Smart Recruiter if I'm new to X-Ray Search?
• Our X-Ray Search and Boolean Strints Generator tools doesn't require any experience with boolean operators.
• No need to put quotation marks, AND/OR//NOT, minus or brackets (we do that for you).
Where does the data come from?
• The results are public.
• All the records have been indexed by Google.
• It's completly legal to use Boolean and X-Ray on your daily searches.
How can I get better results?
• We recommend to start your searches with simple keywords and try to be as specific as possible. After getting used to the tool you can build more complex searches.
• For better results be creative and play with synonymes.
• Type in English, but also make your search in your native language.
• Save your best searches and recheck the results from time to time for new leads.
